Who We Are

Heavenly Treatz Bakels is a small bakery business that focuses on making delicious cakes and baked treats for different occasions.

We believe that every celebration deserves something sweet, beautiful, and memorable. That is why we work hard to prepare cakes that look good and taste good.

What We Do

We make different types of cakes such as customized basic cakes, wedding cakes, character cakes, cupcakes, cakesicles, and bento packages.

We also sell baking ingredients, utensils, and equipment for people who enjoy baking at home or for business.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring happiness to our customers through quality cakes, useful baking supplies, and good service.

We want Heavenly Treatz Bakels to be a trusted name when people need cakes and baking products for special moments.

More Than Just a Bakery

Heavenly Treatz Bakels is not only about selling cakes. We also offer baking lessons for people who want to learn how to bake and decorate cakes.

Our goal is to help customers enjoy both the products we make and the baking knowledge we share.
